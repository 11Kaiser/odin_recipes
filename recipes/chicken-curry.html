Filipino Chicken Curry

Description

Chicken Curry stewed in coconut milk and curry spices is a hearty and tasty dish the whole family will love. Easy to make and cooks in one pan!

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1/4 tablespoon garlic salt
  • 4 skinless, boneles chicken breast halves
  • 3 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon chicken bouillon granules
  • 1 teaspoon ground thyme
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 potato, cube
  • 1 green bell pepper, choope

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a saucepan over medium-high heat. Stir in onion, garlic, ginger, and garlic salt. Add chicken breasts. Mix in curry powder; cook chicken until white, about 5 minutes. Add chicken bouillon and thyme.
  2. Pour 2 cups water into the saucepan once chicken has browned. Add potatoes and simmer until softened, about 10 minutes. Stir in green bell pepper and simmer 5 more minutes. Mix well.
